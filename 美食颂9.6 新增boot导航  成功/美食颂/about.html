company profile

美食颂(北京)餐饮服务有限公司以团餐为主营业态，品牌化连锁经营；搭建了以B2B+B2C为模式的双轮驱动平台，为政府机关、企事业单位、大中专院校、医院、工厂员工餐厅和国家驻外机构，提供整体膳食解决方案。是一家集餐饮服务、餐饮投资、管理、咨询、为一体的综合性专业餐饮服务公司。

Food Sing (Beijing) Food and Beverage Service Co., Ltd. takes group meal business as the main business and adopts brand-oriented chain-store operations. The company has built a B2B+B2C driven platform to provide integrated dietary solutions for staff canteens and dining halls of state organs, public institutions, colleges, secondary schools, military academies, hospitals, enterprises and factories. The company is a comprehensive food and beverage service company that integrates food and beverage service, food and beverage investment, management and consulting.
